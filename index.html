About Us

Che Zau was founded as a small restaurant in London in 1979 by MingCong, Tyler Wang, Ruiying Zhang, and Shawn Pang. Their top priority was to ensure customer satisfaction by providing the freshest food and the best service. In 1986, they changed their restaurant to buffet-style to accommodate the long line-ups of customers waiting to try their much talked about cuisine. They continuously maintained their commitment to customer satisfaction. This commitment is the basis of the company’s mission statement: “Every customer must feel welcomed as soon as they arrive. Every customer must be happy and smiling when they leave”.
